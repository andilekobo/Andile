Our services



Plumber

Our plumbers are sourced from local communities, ensuring that even disadvantaged areas receive top-notch plumbing services. We rigorously vet each professional to maintain high standards of quality and reliability.

House Worker

We connect with local agencies and community groups to find dedicated house workers. These workers are carefully selected to ensure they provide excellent service, bringing their skills to areas that need them most.

Baby Sitter

Our babysitters come from trusted community networks, providing peace of mind for parents in disadvantaged areas. Each sitter is thoroughly background-checked and trained to offer safe, reliable childcare.

Grass Cutter

We employ grass cutters from local neighborhoods, helping to keep disadvantaged areas well-maintained. Our grass cutters are selected based on their commitment to quality and their ability to deliver dependable service.
